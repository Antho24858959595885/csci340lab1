What to Expect

No stress! no talk about work! get ready to decompress and have fun with your coworkers. Live local music, games, and a fun atmosphere!

Why the Cookout?

My three random words were cream, weather, and beef, so I decided to turn them into an invitation for a cookout. Here's why!

Cream

Cream connects to the dessert part of the cookout (ice cream, cake for a 4th of July party, etc).

Weather

Weather connects to the outdoor part of the cookout and the sunny poolside theme.

Beef

Beef connects to the grill, usually serving foods like burgers or steak.
